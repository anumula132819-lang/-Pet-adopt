How It Works

Simple. Thoughtful. Joyful.

We've designed every step of the adoption journey to be effortless, so you can focus on what matters — finding your companion.

01
Browse & Discover

Explore hundreds of pets filtered by type, age, size, and location. Every profile tells their unique story.

02
Find Your Match

Read their personality, health status, and background. Save favourites and compare at your own pace.

03
Apply Online

A short, friendly application form. No paperwork piles. We review within 24 hours and connect you with the shelter.

04

Meet your new companion. Our team provides post-adoption support to help everyone settle in beautifully.

Our Mission

We believe every animal
deserves a loving home.

Founded in 2019, Paws & Home was built to remove the friction from pet adoption and replace it with warmth, clarity, and trust.

Who We Are

Built by animal lovers,
designed for families.

We started with a simple belief: adopting a pet should feel as joyful as having one. Too many great animals sat in shelters while families struggled through outdated, frustrating processes.

So we built something better — a platform that is calm, clear, and full of heart.
